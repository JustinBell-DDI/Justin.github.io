Here is my wireframe that I have made for the sketch of this very webpage. I knew that I wanted to put a picture of the sketch before I had finished making the wireframe, so I drew a picture of itself within itself, which has a picture of itself, again and again, recursively.

I liked being able to make things from scratch using HTML, and experimenting with the colors and dimensions using CSS. I initially struggled with placing things where I wanted them, such as the text box this text is enclosed in, but eventually I figured it out.
